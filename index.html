About Us

Who We Are

At OMICROBEX, we are driven by a passion for advancing scientific discovery and innovation. Founded by a team of dedicated professionals with a shared vision, we have cultivated an environment that fosters creativity, expertise, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Recognizing the significance of the complete sequencing of the human genome, we've joined forces with research companies in India and the United States of America. OMICROBEX boasts a wealth of expertise across various scientific disciplines. From molecular biology to data analytics, our collective knowledge fuels our ability to offer comprehensive solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower researchers, clinicians, and professionals in the medical and health sciences field to push the boundaries of knowledge and make lasting contributions to humanity. We are committed to providing cutting-edge services that elevate research, improve patient care, and facilitate the exchange of vital information.

With a strong commitment to precision, innovation, and collaboration, we offer a diverse range of specialized services that cater to the intricate needs of the scientific community.

What We Do

Information Dissemination: At Omicrobex, we stand for the free and unbridled exchange of curated scientific knowledge. Our commitment lies in disseminating detailed and accurate data about human genes, proteins, metabolites, and transcripts to the worldwide scientific community. This rich repository of information is made available online and is also reflected in our publications in distinguished scientific journals.

Educational Outreach: Our zeal for education and knowledge-sharing is palpable. Omicrobex is dedicated to training scientists from India and across the globe, acquainting them with the latest advancements and methodologies in genomic, proteomic, metabolomic, and transcriptomic research. This includes organizing sessions with globally recognized researchers and fostering an environment ripe for collaborative research.

Biomarker Exploration: Omicrobex ventures into the identification and understanding of biomarkers in multifaceted diseases such as cancers, neurological disorders, and metabolic ailments. We harness cutting-edge techniques like Next Generation Sequencing, Microarray Analysis, and Mass Spectrometry for our studies, ensuring comprehensive coverage of potential biomarkers.

Forging Collaborations: With a global perspective, Omicrobex is continuously on the lookout to forge and sustain partnerships with leading research entities both in India and the USA. This collaborative approach ensures that we remain at the pinnacle of multi-omics research innovations.
